## What it is
The Aethon signature: warm living land (west) draining WEST->EAST to grey, fog-bound, corrupted Blight (east) - colour, light, fog and soundscape all shift CONTINUOUSLY, not at a hard line.

## References to drop
A healthy->corrupted transition; the colour drain; the fog + dead-air mood of the corrupted zone.

## Likely build path
A west->east material/biome blend + a render-baseline lighting shift + a fog-density ramp + Blight vfx + audio ducking.
